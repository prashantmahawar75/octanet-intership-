SHELBY TIME !

The Shelby family is a tight-knit clan at the center of the Peaky Blinders saga, known for their cunning intelligence and ruthlessness in the criminal underworld. Led by the enigmatic Tommy Shelby, the family navigates the post-World War I landscape of Birmingham with a mix of street smarts and strategic planning. Each member, from the hot-headed Arthur to the shrewd Polly, brings a unique strength and complexity to the family dynamic, making them a force to be reckoned with in the gritty world they inhabit.

Despite their outward appearance of unity and strength, the Shelby family is plagued by inner turmoil and personal demons. Tommy, haunted by his experiences in the war, grapples with his own morality and the weight of leadership. Arthur battles with addiction and a longing for redemption, while Polly struggles with her traumatic past and the challenges of being a woman in a male-dominated world. Their individual struggles and conflicts add layers of depth to the family's story, showing that even the strongest of clans have their vulnerabilities and fractures.
